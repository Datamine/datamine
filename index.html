TV 1950-2014 Episode Summary Dataset & Analysis November 4, 2014
Wikipedia has episode summaries for television shows that aired in the US between 1950 and 2014. Scraping these summaries yields large textual datasets, which can be analyzed for changes in televised content over time, which in turn suggest sociological conclusions. I published some sociological analyses, along with datasets, analytical tools, and Wikipedia scrapers.
